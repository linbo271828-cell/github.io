A quantitative trading-agent project framed around decision-making under uncertainty. Key pieces include:

  • Decision structure: cost-aware band/control logic; exploration of POMDP-style framing.
  • Engineering: tick-accurate simulator with transaction costs, inventory, and reproducible experiments.
  • Evaluation: planned comparisons vs. no-trade and buy/hold baselines (paired seeds).
